· Realtime in Android   

The preliminary numbers about Androids real-time performance vary considerably, depending on the source. In general, though, when the application is relatively simple and the system is lightly loaded with no more than 1 to 5 interrupts per second, and the frequency of interrupts is in the 10 - 30 Hertz range, the number of deadline misses are troubling, but manageable. But when the system is operating under heavy loads with a dozen or so interrupts per second, and the frequency of interrupts is in the 1 to 5 kilohertz range, the Android OS performance degrades drastically.
